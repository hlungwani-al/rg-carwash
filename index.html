What We Do?

Premium Washing Services

Exterior Washing

At RG-CarWash, we cleanse your car's exterior with eco-friendly products, leaving it gleaming and shining like a polished gem.

Interior Washing

At RG-CarWash, we deep-clean your car's interior, removing dirt, stains, and odors to restore its pristine condition. Our meticulous approach ensures your car's interior is as fresh and clean as the day you bought it.

Vacuum Cleaning

Our powerful vacuums remove even the tiniest particles, leaving your car’s interior spotless and free of debris. At RG-CarWash, we ensure your car is as clean as can be.

Seats Washing

Our expert cleaning techniques gently remove stains and revitalize your car's seats, leaving them looking and smelling as good as new.

Window Wiping

Our skilled team provides thorough window wiping services, eliminating streaks and smudges to ensure maximum visibility and clear views for a safe and enjoyable ride.

Wet Cleaning

Our specialized wet cleaning process gently yet effectively removes stubborn dirt, grime, and stains, leaving your car's interior surfaces sparkling clean and refreshed.

Oil Changing

At RG-CarWash, our skilled technicians provide fast, efficient oil changing services to keep your car running smoothly. We ensure your car's engine is properly lubricated and protected for optimal performance

Brake Reparing

When it comes to brake repair, our certified mechanics are here to diagnose and fix any issues, ensuring your car's safety and performance on the road.
